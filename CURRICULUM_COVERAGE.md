## 2. Engine Specialisation Map

### 1. Integer Engine / Number Line
- **Focus:** Basic number lines, integer ordering, absolute values, jumps, positive/negative directions.

### 2. Fraction Engine
- **Focus:** Beginner (interactive sector slicing) and Advanced (direct numerator input) modes across 5 different representations (Circle, Bar, Number Line, Area Model, Set Model).

### 3. Algebra Engine
- **Focus:** Algebra tiles ($+x$, $-x$, $+1$, $-1$), seesaw scale balancing, expression builder workspaces, and linear step solvers.

### 4. Graph Engine (Coordinate & Stats)
- **Focus:** Cartesian grids, zoom/pan bounds, lines, rays, vectors, distance/midpoint calculations, and 6 standard chart forms (Bar, Histogram, Pie, Scatter, Line, Frequency Polygon).
